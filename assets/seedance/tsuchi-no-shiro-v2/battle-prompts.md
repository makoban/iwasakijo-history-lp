# 合戦カット強化プロンプト

## Battle 01: 土橋の詰まり

参照画像: `cut-06-earth-bridge-bottleneck.png`

Use @画像1 as the visual reference and first frame. Create a 5-second cinematic Sengoku-period battle shot at Iwasaki Castle, focused on the narrow earth bridge and timber gate bottleneck.

The scene must feel powerful and immediate: attackers rush into the cramped passage below the earth rampart, spears collide, wooden palisades shake, dust and mud rise, defenders hold the high ground above the dry moat. Low camera close to feet, mud, spear shafts, and the timber gate, then a strong push forward into the pressure of the bottleneck.

Historically grounded Japanese 1584 battlefield, earthwork castle, dry moat, earth rampart, wooden palisades, timber gate, plain cloth banners. No tenshu, no stone keep, no fantasy armor, no modern objects. No readable text, no subtitles, no kanji, no family crests, no logos. Keep banners plain. Powerful but not graphic: no excessive blood, no gore.

No music. If sound is generated, use only sound effects: running feet, shouted commands, spear impacts, wood creaking, dust, breath, wind.

## Battle 02: 木柵際の衝突

参照画像: `cut-07-palisade-clash.png`

Make this the most intense battle cut: close camera at chest height beside the palisade, spears thrust and press through gaps in the wooden fence, warriors strain and shout, dust bursts from the earth rampart, wood splinters, cloth banners whip in the wind. Add controlled handheld camera energy and a strong forward surge, but keep the action readable and grounded.

Historically grounded late 16th century Japanese battlefield, small earthwork castle, dry moat, muddy slope, wooden palisades, plain cloth banners, ashigaru and samurai retainers. No tenshu, no stone keep, no fantasy armor, no modern objects. No readable text, no subtitles, no kanji, no family crests, no logos. Banners must be plain with no symbols. Powerful but not graphic: no excessive blood, no gore.

No music. If sound is generated, use only sound effects: battle shouts, spear impacts, wood cracking, footwork, armor movement, dust, wind.
